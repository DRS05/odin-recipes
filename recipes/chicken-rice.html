Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This recipe is perfect for off-days where you want a quick, cheap and delicious meal! Has a perfect combination of rice, chicken, soy and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat egg and water together in small bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat; add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds.
  4. Heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
  5. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in shredded egg.
  7. Serve hot!
